Hello Wrold !

I am Hugo Parischewsky Zapata

As a recent graduate with a Bachelor's degree in Astrophysics from the Universidad de Concepción, my academic journey has been fueled by an unwavering passion for unraveling the mysteries of the cosmos. My fascination lies in harnessing the power of data visualization to unearth intricate correlations concealed within experimental findings. Presently, I am immersed in a captivating role as a researcher within the Astronomy department at my alma mater, collaborating closely with a dynamic theory group. Here, I engage in the meticulous realm of numerical integrations, orchestrating celestial dynamics experiments that unveil the secrets of the universe.

With a resolute focus, I am drawn towards the prospect of advancing my academic pursuits through a Master's or Ph.D. program that intersects celestial dynamics, numerical integrations, and the application of computer science to the realm of astronomy.

The boundless potential of data visualization has captivated me, offering a potent lens to decipher the enigmatic behavior of dynamic systems. I ardently believe that through visualizing data, we can illuminate novel connections within experimental outcomes, propelling our understanding to new frontiers.

About me

Bringing Astronomy to the Community

My deep fascination with the field of astronomical observation and astrophotography has been an enduring flame since my early academic years.

As a second year student, I immersed myself fully in the different types of telescopes that the Astronomy Department of the Universidad de Concepción owned.A defining facet of my involvement has been the collaborative orchestration of enlightening discussions, generously accessible to the public. These enriching dialogues, along with our initiatives encompassing educational forays to rural schools and municipalities, marked a stage in which I became fully engaged in fostering the understanding and appreciation of astronomy and the night sky.

Personally one of the things I most enjoyed during this stage was the integration of our community with celestial wonders, meticulously orchestrating captivating displays of solar and lunar eclipses in venues of significant community congregation.

Live streams

I have this huge love for space, and I'm really excited to share it with all of you. That's why I thought of creating a cool Twitch channel. It's like a special online spot where I can show you awesome things happening in the sky through my telescope, right as they're happening. The channel is getting bigger and bigger, and it's a welcoming place where we can all discover cool facts together.

We'll talk about things like stars, planets, how they're born, grow, and eventually fade away. I'm totally thrilled to answer any questions you might have whether they're about space stuff, the tools I use, or the latest space news. It's pretty cool to realize that the things we can see up there change every month, like a big cosmic story unfolding bit by bit.

Currently, I'm using a really strong telescope called eVscope Equinox to show you live views. It's like a magical window into the universe that we can all enjoy together, right here online!

So come along, join me on this awesome new journey!

Outdoor activities

Chile, my home country, boasts a geography defined by its elongated and narrow shape, which significantly influences its weather patterns. The diverse landscapes, rich traditions, architectural marvels, and captivating narratives weave together into a vibrant tapestry.

During my upbringing, I found myself entranced by the allure of the southern regions. I frequently embarked on journeys to spots nestled close to the mountains, where I would spend weeks camping and immersing myself in the beauty of natural landscapes. Each excursion demanded distinct physical capabilities, as well as a sense of tranquility and patience – a remarkable opportunity to enhance my knowledge and self-control.

This innate passion organically drove me to arrange group camping expeditions and captivating stargazing sessions beneath the night sky. As my educational pursuits advanced, this inclination evolved into a cherished avenue for sharing the knowledge I was acquiring during my academic journey. These experiences stand as some of the most valuable and rewarding moments of my life.
